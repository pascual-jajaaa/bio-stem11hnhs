Fluid Connective Tissue

primarily in transportation of substances such as oxygen, nutrients, hormones, and waste products throughout the body.

Types of Fluid Connective Tissue

Blood

  • blood cells suspended in plasma
  • RBC, WBC and platelets
  • transport food, oxygen, waste, hormones

Lymph

  • clear fluid that circulates through the lymphatic system
  • lymphocytes and other immune cells
