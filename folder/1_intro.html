Introduction

In the study of human anatomy, connective tissue plays a pivotal role as it serves as a protective structural framework for various tissues throughout the body. Building on the foundational knowledge of epithelial tissue, this website delves into the intricacies of connective tissue types, their features, and funcitons. The three principal categories of connective tissue include connective tissue proper, structural connective tissue, and fluid connective tissue. Each type exhibits distinct characteristics and serves varied functions beyond mere connectivity, including binding, support, protection, insulation, storage, and transportation.
